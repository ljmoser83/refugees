New Additions to the Melting Pot:
State Refugee Totals by Nationality
2008-2016

This interactive choropleth map shows the total number of refugees each state receives by year. This map was developed in response to the impending travel ban in the United States. Use this map to explore the current refugee controversy and better understand the diversity and value that refugees add to our country. The specific countries that are subject to potential bans are some of the countries in the world with the greatest number of people seeking refuge. By implementing a wholesale block on these countries, we may be simply ignoring folks that need help the most.

Interpreting the Map

The states are symbolized with color coded jenks natural breaks clustering method. Color coded groups show the magnitude of refugees each state receives on a yearly basis. Consult the legend as a guide to this symbology. You can also click on each state to retrieve a pop-up window showing the number of refugees by nationality for a given state of interest. The included graph shows the trend of total refugees received by state across the time frame.
